# Area Pop Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Area Pop does not collect, store, share, or sell personal information.

The app does not include third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, account systems, sign-in features, or server-side gameplay services.

Gameplay data, such as the best score, is stored only on the user's device. This data is not transmitted to the developer or to any third party.

Area Pop does not track users across apps or websites.

If advertising, analytics, online services, or other third-party SDKs are added in a future version, this privacy policy and the App Store privacy details will be updated before that version is submitted for review.
